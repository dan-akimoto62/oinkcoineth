HOW TO BUY

4 STEPS

1. Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or any Solana wallet from the App Store, Google Play, or as a Chrome extension.

2. Get Some SOL

Fund your wallet with SOL to swap for $OINK. Buy SOL from an exchange or use a cross-chain swap.

3. Go to Raydium

Visit Raydium, connect your wallet, and get ready to trade $OINK.

Exchange your SOL for $OINK on Raydium in just a few clicks. Enjoy fast and low-cost transactions.
